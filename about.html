About Me

Hi, I'm John Dave!
Wanna know me more?

Hello! My name is John Dave Partible, and I am 18 years old. I was born on September 5, 2006, in the beautiful province of La Union. I currently reside near Mines View, one of the well-known tourist destinations in Baguio City, but I proudly trace my roots to the municipality of Bangar, La Union.

I am currently pursuing a degree in Criminology at the University of the Cordilleras. As someone who is curious about justice, safety, and community service, I chose this field to better understand crime prevention and law enforcement. I believe education is key to creating safer communities, and I am committed to contributing to that goal through my studies and future work in the field.

Outside of academics, I enjoy engaging in sports that keep me active and focused. My favorite sports include badminton, and basketball. These activities not only serve as a great form of exercise but also help me develop teamwork, discipline, and a competitive spirit.

I come from a large and loving family. I am the youngest among eight siblings, with two older brothers and five sisters. Growing up in such a big household has taught me the value of patience, cooperation, and mutual respect.

My educational journey began at Saint Christopher Academy, where I attended kindergarten. I later transferred to Cadapli Elementary School to complete my elementary education. For junior high school, I studied at Regional Science High School, and eventually transferred to CASACRISTO National High School, where I completed both junior and senior high school.

In senior high, I pursued the TVL Automotive Servicing NCII strand. Through this track, I gained hands-on experience in vehicle diagnostics, engine repair, disassembly and reassembly, and other mechanical procedures. I graduated with honors and was awarded distinctions for both Best in Research and Best in Conduct, reflecting my dedication to both academic excellence and good character.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me. I look forward to sharing more with you through this website!
